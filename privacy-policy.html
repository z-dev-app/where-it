Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Introduction

Welcome to WhereIt. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

Information We Don't Collect

WhereIt is designed with privacy in mind. We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • We do not collect or store your personal information on our servers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your photos beyond what you explicitly select
  • We do not share any of your data with third parties

Information Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Item photos and descriptions
  • Category information
  • Location descriptions
  • Reminder settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up through your personal iCloud account.

App Permissions

WhereIt requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera & Photo Library Access: Required only when you choose to add photos of your items
  • Notifications: Required only if you set reminders for your items

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored securely on your device using iOS's built-in security features
  • iCloud sync uses Apple's end-to-end encryption
  • We do not maintain any servers that store your personal information

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All data storage is local to the device and under parental control through iOS's built-in features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
